Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 10, 2025

Robinson Development Group, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Future Me handles your information.

DATA STORAGE AND ENCRYPTION

Future Me is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All your time capsules are stored locally on your device using strong encryption
  • Your messages are encrypted before being saved to your device's database
  • No personal content is transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Your encryption keys never leave your device

INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your time capsule messages or content
  • Your personal identification information
  • Your device's contacts, photos, or other apps' data
  • Your location information
  • Your browsing habits or app usage patterns

INFORMATION WE MAY COLLECT

For app functionality and improvement, we may collect limited, anonymized data:

  • Crash reports and error logs (no personal content included)
  • Basic app performance metrics
  • General usage statistics (number of capsules created, features used)

AI SUMMARY FEATURE

If you use the optional AI summary feature:

  • Your capsule content is sent to OpenAI's API for summary generation
  • This data is processed according to OpenAI's privacy policy
  • Summaries are encrypted and stored locally on your device
  • You can disable this feature at any time

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app may use these third-party services:

  • OpenAI API for generating summaries (optional feature)
  • App store services for purchases and updates

Each service operates under their own privacy policies.

YOUR RIGHTS AND CONTROL

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data remains on your device unless you explicitly use cloud features
  • You can delete the app and all data at any time
  • You can disable AI features to prevent any external data processing
  • No account registration or personal information is required

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Future Me does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • Industry-standard encryption for all stored data
  • Secure communication protocols for any network requests
  • Regular security updates and patches

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify users of any material changes through app updates. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
